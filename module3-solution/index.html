Chicken

Chicken meat is a type of poultry meat that is widely consumed and is known for its high-quality protein and low fat content. It is considered a healthy meat option due to its lower saturated fat content compared to red meats like beef and lamb. Chicken meat is categorized as white meat and has a lower iron content (0.7 mg vs. 2 mg/100 g) compared to other meats.
